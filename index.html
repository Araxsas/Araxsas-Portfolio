About Me

Hello, my name is Aras and I am a 16-year-old Roblox developer who focuses on creating 3D assets for Roblox games.

I have been working with Roblox Studio for about two years and started learning Blender several months ago.

I mainly use Blender along with various texture and material resources to create props, simple environment assets and game objects.

My work focuses on stylized and optimized models designed to fit well into Roblox games.

I enjoy creating assets for games and improving my skills with every project.

Feel free to take a look at some of my work.
